Online Recruitment


Description

The "Online Recruitment" module extends Odoo's standard Recruitment module's functionality by providing unique features and improving the organization's overall recruiting process. Also, this module provides a platform that facilitates managing online recruitments.

The "Online Recruitment" module allows receiving applications from multiple sources, facilitating the collection and, cleverly choosing the candidate data by providing additional features such as "Experience" and "Educations" tabs.

Key Features

  • Improves your understanding of candidates.
  • Fully integrated with other Odoo applications.
  • Accelerates the hiring process and reduces manual efforts.
  • Ability to search applicants by entered experiences or education data.
  • Integrates candidates' experience or education data with recruitment application.
  • Ability to record applicants' experiences and education data through the application form.
